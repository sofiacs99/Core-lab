Sofia Cacho Sousa

EXPRESSIONS

FORREST
My Momma always told me that
miracles happen every day. Some
people don't think so, but they do.
Jenny and Forrest walk.
A dirt clod hits Forrest in the back of the head.
Three young boys get off their bikes and pick up more rocks.
Forrest is hit in the eye with
another dirt clod. Forrest falls
backward onto the ground as the
boys glare at him.
Jenny helps Forrest back up.
Boy #1 and Boy #2 throw
more dirt clods at Forrest.
